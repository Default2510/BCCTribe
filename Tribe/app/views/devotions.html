A Life Devoted

These guided devotions will help you get started in your Kingdom Living. They are simple audio exercises that can help you find an on ramp for your own. Devotions are simple scripture-based expressions of understanding, encouragement, or practical guidance. The devotions offered here are only a few minutes long so you can do them any time at any place during your day. New devotions are provided seasonally, so be sure to check back often.
