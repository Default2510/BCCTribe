Seek First

We call our morning Bible Studies Seek First because we walking in the command of Jesus to "seek first His kingdom and His righteousness" (Matt 6:33, NIV). When you come to a Seek First group you don't have to expect too much of yourself and others. Just come with a bible and an open mind.
